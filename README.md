# Grimoire of Gaia Spell Integration for Iron's Spellbooks

This mod expands **Iron's Spellbooks** with spells inspired by **Grimoire of Gaia**, allowing you to **summon monster girls** from GoG to fight by your side.

Unlike their non-summon versions, these summoned versions slightly **adjusted AI and attack patterns** for better combat performance and synergy with Iron's Spellbooksâ€™ mechanics.

### **Ender Dragon Girl**

*   Uses **Dragon Breath** and uses teleportation in fighting.
*   When her health drops low, she gains **true invisibility** for a short time after teleporting.

### **Mummy**

*   When hit, summons **Gravemites** to assist in battle.
*   Her attacks inflict the **Blight** status effect.

### **Nine Tails**

*   Casts **Firebolt** projectiles from range when her health is above 75%.
*   Her melee strikes have a chance to trigger **Flaming Strike**, dealing burst fire damage.

### **Yuki Onna**

*   Her attacks inflict the **Chilled** debuff and slow enemies.
*   When near death, she fires **Icicles**.
*   Upon death, she leaves behind a **self-destructing ice statue**.

### **Valkyrie**

*   Occasionally triggers **Divine Smite** when attacking, dealing holy damage to foes.

### **Sludge Girl**

*   When low on health, **slowly regenerates** while in water.
*   Upon death, releases a lingering **poison areacloud**.

### **Werecat**

*   Her attacks cause the **Slowness** effect.

### **Witch**

*   She can cast Magic Missile/Firefly Swarm/Evoker Fang, and throw potions.
